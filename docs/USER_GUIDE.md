## üöÄ Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Via Email**:
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
   - Enter your name, email, and password
   - Password must contain:
     - At least 8 characters
     - One uppercase letter
     - One lowercase letter
     - One number
     - One special character
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Check your email for verification link (optional)

2. **Via Google**:
   - Click "Continue with Google"
   - Select your Google account
   - Authorize Param Adventures
   - You're in!

### Logging In

- Click "Login" in top right
- Enter your email and password
- Or use "Continue with Google"
- Check "Remember me" to stay logged in

### Forgot Password?

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your inbox for reset link
4. Click link (valid for 1 hour)
5. Enter new password
6. Login with new password

## üìù Booking a Trip

### Step 1: Select Trip & Date

1. Browse and choose your trip
2. Click "Book Now"
3. Select available date (if multiple batches)

### Step 2: Login (if not already)

- If you're not logged in, you'll be redirected to login
- After login, you'll return to booking

### Step 3: Enter Guest Details

Provide information for all travelers:

- Full Name (as per ID)
- Age
- Gender
- Emergency Contact
- Special Requirements (dietary, medical)

**For Group Bookings**:

- You can book for up to 10 people
- Enter details for each guest
- One person is the primary contact

### Step 4: Review Booking

Check:

- Trip name and dates
- Number of guests
- Total price breakdown:
  - Base price
  - GST (if applicable)
  - Convenience fee
- Cancellation policy

### Step 5: Payment

1. Click "Proceed to Payment"
2. Razorpay secure checkout opens
3. Choose payment method:
   - **Cards**: Credit/Debit (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Rupay)
   - **UPI**: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
   - **Net Banking**: All major banks
   - **Wallets**: Paytm, MobiKwik, etc.
4. Complete payment
5. Get confirmation email instantly

**Payment Security**:

- All payments are processed via Razorpay (PCI DSS compliant)
- Your card details are never stored on our servers
- 128-bit SSL encryption

### After Booking

You'll receive:

- **Confirmation Email**: Booking ID and trip details
- **SMS**: Booking confirmation (if phone provided)
- **Dashboard Access**: View booking in "My Bookings"

## üéí My Bookings

### Viewing Bookings

- Click your avatar ‚Üí "My Bookings"
- See all your past and upcoming trips

### Booking Status

- **Pending**: Payment successful, awaiting admin approval
- **Confirmed**: Approved by admin, you're good to go!
- **In Progress**: Trip is currently happening
- **Completed**: Trip finished, please leave a review!
- **Cancelled**: Booking cancelled (by you or admin)

### Booking Actions

#### Download Booking Confirmation

- Click "Download PDF"
- Includes booking ID, trip details, guest info
- Carry this during your trip

#### Cancel Booking

1. Open booking details
2. Click "Cancel Booking"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Refund processed as per cancellation policy

**Cancellation Policy** (varies by trip):

- More than 30 days before: 90% refund
- 15-30 days before: 50% refund
- Less than 15 days: No refund
- Check specific trip policy before booking

#### Contact Support

- Click "Need Help?" on booking page
- Send message to support team
- Expect response within 24 hours

A: Absolutely. We use Razorpay (PCI DSS Level 1 certified) for all payments.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**

**Q: Do I get a refund if I cancel?**

**Q: Are meals included?**  
A: Check the "Inclusions" section on the trip page. Most trips include breakfast and dinner.

A: Highly recommended, especially for adventure trips. Not included in trip price.

**Q: What fitness level is required?**

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" error  
**Solution**:

- Double-check email and password
- Ensure caps lock is off
- Try "Forgot Password?" if unsure

**Problem**: Can't receive password reset email  
**Solution**:

- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 5 minutes and try again
- Verify you're using the correct email
- Contact support if issue persists

### Booking Issues

**Problem**: "Book Now" button not working  
**Solution**:

- Ensure you're logged in
- Check if trip has available slots
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache

**Problem**: Payment failed but money deducted  
**Solution**:

- Don't retry payment immediately
- Check "My Bookings" for confirmation
- Money auto-refunded in 5-7 days if booking failed
- Contact support with transaction ID

### Profile Issues

**Problem**: Can't upload profile picture  
**Solution**:

- Image must be under 5MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
- Try compressing the image
- Use a different browser

**Problem**: Changes not saving  
**Solution**:

- Ensure all required fields filled
- Check for error messages
- Try logging out and back in

### General Issues

**Problem**: Website loading slowly  
**Solution**:

- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions

**Problem**: Can't see my bookings  
**Solution**:

- Ensure you're logged into the correct account
- Refresh the page
- Check if booking email was received
- Contact support with booking ID
